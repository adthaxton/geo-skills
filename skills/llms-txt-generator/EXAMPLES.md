## Example 3: Before and After — Improving a Weak llms.txt

**Before (weak — too vague, marketing language, no entity value):**

```
# Best Law Firm

We are the best personal injury lawyers in Texas. Our experienced
team of attorneys fights hard for our clients to get the maximum
compensation they deserve. We offer free consultations.

Contact us today!
```

**After (strong — specific, entity-rich, AI-optimized):**

```
# Holloway Law Firm

> Holloway Law Firm is a personal injury law firm representing
> accident and injury victims across Texas from offices in Dallas,
> Houston, and San Antonio.

Holloway Law Firm was founded in 2005 by James R. Holloway and
specializes in car accident cases, 18-wheeler truck accidents,
workplace injuries, and wrongful death claims. The firm represents
individuals and families against insurance companies and large
corporations.

The firm has recovered over $75 million in settlements and verdicts
for clients. Attorneys at Holloway Law Firm are licensed in Texas
and recognized by Super Lawyers (2019-2026) and Martindale-Hubbell
(AV Preeminent rated).

This website provides legal information for injury victims in Texas,
including guides on the claims process, statute of limitations,
evidence collection, and how to evaluate a personal injury case.
All content is authored or reviewed by licensed Texas attorneys.
```
